Hello there! I'm Aristotelis (or Aristotle or Aris, whichever suits you best). I hold a Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (2020) and a MSc in Artificial Intelligence from KU Leuven (2023). My scientific interests include the fields of Computer Vision, Signal Processing, Deep Learning, Robotics and Natural Language Processing.

Currently, I work as a research assistant at the Centre for Research & Technology Hellas (CERTH), where I am actively engaged in three research projects focusing on the development of Computer Vision, Signal Processing and Natural Language Processing components.

Known for my adaptability and problem-solving skills, combined with a dash of hard work, a sprinkling of positive thinking, and a good dose of humor. In my free time, you'll often find me grooving to the rhythm of my sax, strumming my guitar with the band, or indulging my love for acting.
